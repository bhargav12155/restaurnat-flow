About imakepage

imakepage is a professional real estate website builder designed for real estate agents, teams, and homebuilders.

What does imakepage do?

imakepage allows real estate professionals to:

  • Create stunning, customizable websites in minutes
  • Showcase property listings with advanced search features
  • Generate and manage leads from potential buyers and sellers
  • Connect with clients through integrated contact forms
  • Customize their website appearance with themes and colors
  • Use custom domains for their professional presence

Who uses imakepage?

  • Individual real estate agents
  • Real estate teams and brokerages
  • Homebuilders and developers

Key Features

  • Property listing integration
  • Lead generation tools
  • Mobile responsive design
  • SEO optimization
  • Custom domain support
  • Template customization
