## 🎯 Step-by-Step Creation Process

### Phase 1: Create PowerPoint Presentation (15 minutes)

1. **Open PowerPoint** and create new presentation
2. **Copy content** from `powerpoint-slides-content.md`
3. **Apply design** using the color scheme and fonts specified
4. **Create 6 slides** as outlined:
   - Title slide
   - AI Content transition  
   - Social Media transition
   - SEO transition
   - Analytics transition
   - Call to action slide

### Phase 2: Record App Segments (20 minutes)

1. **Prepare your browser:**
   - Open `http://localhost:5000` in full screen
   - Clear bookmarks bar and other distractions
   - Use 1080p resolution for crisp recording

2. **Record each segment** (using screen recording software):

   **Segment 1: Dashboard Overview (15 seconds)**
   - Show main dashboard with sidebar
   - Hover over key metrics
   - Smooth navigation movements

   **Segment 2: AI Content Generation (35 seconds)**
   - Navigate to AI Content Generator
   - Type: "New listing in Benson neighborhood - 3BR ranch home with updated kitchen"
   - Add keywords: "Benson real estate, Omaha homes, ranch style"
   - Click Generate and show results

   **Segment 3: Social Media Manager (35 seconds)**
   - Paste AI content into social media manager
   - Check Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, YouTube platforms
   - Upload the test video file (`demo-property-tour.mp4`)
   - Click "Post Now" and show success

   **Segment 4: SEO & Calendar (25 seconds)**
   - Quick SEO keyword addition
   - Show content calendar with scheduled posts

### Phase 3: Video Editing (30 minutes)

1. **Import all materials** into your video editor (CapCut, iMovie, or DaVinci Resolve)
2. **Arrange timeline:**
   - Title slide (10s)
   - AI transition slide (5s) + AI recording (35s)
   - Social transition slide (5s) + Social recording (35s)  
   - SEO transition slide (5s) + SEO recording (20s)
   - Analytics transition slide (5s) + Analytics recording (20s)
   - CTA slide (15s)

3. **Apply effects:**
   - Speed up recordings to 1.5x-2x
   - Add smooth transitions between segments
   - Overlay captions for key actions
   - Add subtle background music

4. **Export settings:**
   - Format: MP4 (H.264)
   - Resolution: 1920x1080
   - Frame rate: 30fps  
   - Bitrate: 8-12 Mbps

## 💡 Pro Tips for Best Results

### Recording Tips:
- Record at normal speed, speed up in editing
- Use deliberate, smooth mouse movements
- Wait for animations and page loads to complete
- Record each segment 2-3 times to get the best take

### Editing Tips:
- Keep transitions quick (0.3-0.5 seconds)
- Use arrows or circles to highlight key UI elements
- Add captions for accessibility
- Keep background music subtle (20-30% volume)

### Branding Tips:
- Use consistent colors from the PowerPoint slides
- Include "My Golden Brick LLC" branding
- Add Mike Bjork contact information in the CTA
- Maintain professional tone throughout
