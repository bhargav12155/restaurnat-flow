## PowerPoint Design Guidelines

### Color Scheme:
- Primary Blue: #667eea
- Secondary Purple: #764ba2  
- Accent Orange: #ff6b6b
- Text Dark: #2d3748
- Text Light: #718096

### Fonts:
- Headlines: Montserrat Bold (32-48pt)
- Subheadings: Montserrat Semibold (24-28pt)
- Body Text: Open Sans Regular (18-20pt)
- Captions: Open Sans Regular (14-16pt)

### Layout Guidelines:
- Consistent margins: 1 inch on all sides
- Icon size: 64px x 64px for section headers
- Logo placement: Top right corner (small) or center (large)
- Bullet points: Use checkmarks (✓) for benefits
- Maintain 3-4 lines max per slide for readability

### Animation Suggestions:
- Slide transitions: Fade (0.5 seconds)
- Text entrance: Fly in from left (0.3 seconds)
- Icons: Zoom in (0.2 seconds)
- Keep animations subtle and professional

### Branding Elements:
- My Golden Brick LLC logo (if available)
- Berkshire Hathaway HomeServices colors as accents
- Professional real estate imagery
- Consistent typography throughout
