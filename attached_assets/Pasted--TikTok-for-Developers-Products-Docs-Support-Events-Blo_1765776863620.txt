Application Overview - imakepage.com TikTok Integration

PLATFORM DESCRIPTION:
imakepage.com is a comprehensive real estate marketing platform that empowers real estate agents, homebuilders, and property professionals to establish and grow their online presence. Our platform provides users with customizable website templates, property listing management tools, and integrated marketing capabilities designed specifically for the real estate industry.

PURPOSE OF TIKTOK INTEGRATION:
The TikTok API integration serves as a critical component of our social media marketing suite, enabling real estate professionals to expand their reach and engage with potential homebuyers on one of the fastest-growing social media platforms. With over 1 billion active users, TikTok has become an essential channel for real estate marketing, particularly for reaching younger first-time homebuyers and millennial audiences who actively use the platform for home search and inspiration.
